About Aditya Ayurved

Manufacturing Quality Ayurvedic Formulations for Over a Decade

Based in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, Aditya Ayurved has been manufacturing Ayurvedic syrups and tablets since 2011. We produce a wide range of formulations — from digestive enzymes and liver tonics to pain relief and women's wellness syrups — for retailers, distributors and wholesale buyers across India.

Every batch is produced in-house in our ISO 9001:2015 and GMP certified Meerut facility, giving us direct control over formulation, packaging and quality.
