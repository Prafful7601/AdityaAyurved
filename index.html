About Aditya Ayurved

Manufacturing Quality Ayurvedic Formulations for Over a Decade

Based in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, Aditya Ayurved has been manufacturing Ayurvedic syrups and tablets since 2011. We produce a wide range of formulations — from digestive enzyme syrups and liver tonics to women's wellness syrups and tablets — for retailers, distributors and wholesale buyers across India.

Every batch is produced in-house in our Meerut facility, giving us direct control over formulation, packaging and quality.
