Our Story

A Decade-Plus of Ayurvedic Manufacturing

Aditya Ayurved was established in 2011 in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, with a simple goal — to manufacture honest, well-made Ayurvedic formulations using traditional ingredients and processes.

Over the years, our range has grown to 29 formulations across syrups and tablets, covering digestive care, liver care, women's wellness, pain & joint care and specialty Ayurvedic care. We work directly with retailers, distributors and wholesale buyers across India, manufacturing every batch in-house at our ISO 9001:2015 and GMP certified Meerut facility.
