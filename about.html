Our Story

A Decade-Plus of Ayurvedic Manufacturing

Aditya Ayurved was established in 2011 in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, with a simple goal — to manufacture honest, well-made Ayurvedic formulations using traditional ingredients and processes.

Over the years, our range has grown to 17 formulations across syrups and tablets, covering digestive enzymes, liver tonics, women's wellness, cough relief and general Ayurvedic care. We work directly with retailers, distributors and wholesale buyers across India, manufacturing every batch in-house at our Meerut facility.
